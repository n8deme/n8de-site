Cookie policy

Information about cookies and trackers used on n8de.me

Last updated: April 28, 2026

01.What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file placed on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when visiting a website. It allows the site to memorize information about your visit (preferred language, session data, etc.) to facilitate your subsequent navigation.

This policy informs you about the cookies used on the n8de.me website, their purpose, and how you can control them.

02.Our approach

n8de.me adopts a minimalist approach to cookies.

We do not use advertising cookies, nor profiling cookies for commercial purposes. No data is sold to third parties.

We only use cookies strictly necessary for the site's operation and, optionally, anonymized audience measurement cookies to improve our service.

03.Categories of cookies used

3.1. Essential cookies (always active)

These cookies are essential for the proper functioning of the site. They cannot be disabled without impacting your navigation.

Name Purpose Duration Type
session Maintenance of user session during navigation Session Essential
cookieconsent Memorization of your cookie choice 12 months Essential

3.2. Audience measurement cookies (consent required)

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our site, in an anonymized manner. They are only deposited with your explicit consent.

Name Purpose Duration Type
_ga (if active) Anonymous audience measurement (Google Analytics with anonymized IP) 13 months Analytics
_ga_* (if active) Differentiate unique users for statistics 13 months Analytics

Note: if you see these cookies in your browser, it means they are effectively deposited. As of the policy update date, their activation depends on the consent module.

04.Managing your consent

4.1. On your first visit

On your first visit, a banner informs you of the use of non-essential cookies and offers you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Refuse non-essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences

Your choice is memorized for 12 months. After that, you will be asked again.

4.2. Modify your preferences

You can modify your preferences at any time:

  • By deleting cookies via your browser settings
  • By returning to the homepage and clicking "Manage cookies" (if available)
  • By contacting us at hello@n8de.me

4.3. Block cookies via your browser

You can directly block or delete cookies from your browser settings. Here are the official guides:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Warning: blocking all cookies may prevent some site features from working properly.

05.Third-party cookies

When you interact with certain integrated content (YouTube videos, LinkedIn demos, Calendly forms, etc.), third-party cookies may be deposited directly by these services. n8de.me has no direct control over these cookies.

For more information, consult the respective privacy policies:

  • Google / YouTube
  • LinkedIn
  • Calendly

06.Retention period

Cookies have a limited lifespan:

  • Session cookies: deleted when the browser is closed
  • Persistent cookies: maximum 13 months (CNIL/APD recommendation)

07.Your rights

In accordance with GDPR and the Belgian law of July 30, 2018, you have rights over your personal data. To exercise them, consult our Privacy Policy or contact us at hello@n8de.me.

You may also file a complaint with the Belgian Data Protection Authority:

Belgian Data Protection Authority

Rue de la Presse 35, 1000 Brussels, Belgium

Website: www.dataprotectionauthority.be

08.Modifications

n8de.me reserves the right to modify this cookie policy at any time, particularly to adapt to legal, technical, or service evolutions. Any modification will be published on this page with an updated last-modified date.

09.Contact

For any question regarding our cookie policy, contact us at: hello@n8de.me
